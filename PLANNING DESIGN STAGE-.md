# ***PLANNING DESIGN STAGE-***



### PROJECT-



Modular Smart Navigation Assist for the Visually Impaired



A clip on device for a white cane(i.e. device used by blind ppl).//UPDATED TO WIRST

##### 

##### MATERIALS NEEDED-





* 4 HC-SR04 ULTRASOUND SENSORS (front, left, right , down\[FOR STAIRS])
* ESP 32 DEV BOARD
* 3 VIBRATOR MOTORS-

  * Left object- Left motor
  * Right object- Right motor
  * Front object- Front motor
  * Down (stairs)- ALL Both long beep and long pause

&#x09;



##### Attachment Method-

&#x09;

Uses screws to attach to any cane.
